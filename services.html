‚úàÔ∏è Student Visa Application Support

We provide end-to-end assistance for student visa applications to leading international study destinations including the UK, Europe, Canada, Australia, and the US. Our expert team ensures students understand the process, meet requirements, and submit strong, error-free applications.

Our Service Include:

  • Visa Process Guidance:

    Clear explanation of the student visa process for each country, including visa types, eligibility, timelines, and key steps.

  • Document Checklist & Evaluation:

    Country-specific documentation guidance with thorough review and vetting of academic, financial, and personal documents required for visa approval.

  • Application Form Assistance:

    Step-by-step support in completing visa application forms accurately for each destination, minimising risks of delay or rejection.

  • Financial Proof Preparation:

    Guidance on meeting financial requirements (bank statements, sponsorships, education loans, etc.) and presenting them in the required format.

  • CAS/I-20/LOA Review:

    Support in obtaining and reviewing key documents such as the Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies (UK), I-20 (US), or Letter of Acceptance (Canada, Australia, EU), ensuring alignment with visa forms.

  • Biometrics & Appointment Booking:

    Assistance with scheduling appointments for biometrics, visa interviews, and document submission at the appropriate visa application centres.

  • Visa Fee & Health Insurance Payments:

    Support in completing payments for visa fees, immigration health surcharges, and mandatory insurance (where applicable).

  • Interview Preparation (if required):

    Coaching and mock interview sessions for countries that require a visa interview, such as the US or UK (credibility interview).

  • Application Tracking & Updates:

    Monitoring application status and timelines, providing regular updates, and advising on next steps post-decision.

  • Post-Visa Guidance:

    Advising students on travel readiness, visa conditions, BRP/permit collection, and compliance with immigration rules upon arrival.
