Work Options for Students in the USA

F1 visa students can explore limited employment opportunities while studying in the U.S. Here are some available paths to consider:

On-Campus Jobs

Available within university premises. A good start for new students.

OPT (Optional Practical Training)

Work off-campus in your field of study after completing your degree.

CPT (Curricular Practical Training)

Gain practical experience through internships during your course.

Economic Hardship

With special permission, work off-campus due to financial needs.

International Organizations

Work opportunities at certain recognized international entities.
